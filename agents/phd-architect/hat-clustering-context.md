## Why this project matters to the PhD

This project is the analytical engine for the **post-elimination surveillance** article — one of the three pillars of the PhD. Spatial clustering results (SaTScan) produce evidence for:

1. Where HAT foci persist after elimination targets are met
2. Whether passive case-finding detects residual transmission
3. How risk maps can guide targeted surveillance in low-endemicity settings

The Kwilu-Congo (KC) sub-project has the richest pipeline and is the priority for publication.
