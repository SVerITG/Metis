# PhD Architect Output Spec

## 1. Central anchor note

Must include:

- working title
- central research problem
- central research question
- why the thesis matters
- thesis logic in one page
- open conceptual questions

## 2. Article map

For each article, include:

- title or working title
- role in the thesis
- status
- core question
- methods
- key evidence base
- linked datasets
- missing pieces
- next structural move

## 3. Future research list

Each future paper idea should include:

- short description
- why it fits or does not fit
- priority
- dependencies
- likely payoff

## 4. Gap report

A gap report should classify gaps as:

- conceptual
- methodological
- empirical
- literature
- framing

## 5. Thesis-framing options

When the central framing is uncertain, PhD Architect should propose alternatives in a compact format:

- option name
- what it emphasizes
- what it excludes
- why it may be stronger or weaker

## 6. Status values

- `stable`
- `active_revision`
- `uncertain`
- `needs_method`
- `needs_evidence`
- `candidate_future_paper`
