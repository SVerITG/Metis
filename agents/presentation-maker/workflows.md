# Presentation Maker Workflows

## 1. Deck-creation workflow

Inputs:

- topic
- audience
- purpose
- available materials

Process:

1. Define the objective.
2. Define the audience.
3. Define the main message.
4. Choose the minimal narrative sequence.
5. Create a slide-by-slide outline.

## 2. Scientific presentation workflow

For scientific decks:

- clarify the research question
- present only the methods needed to understand the findings
- focus the results on the takeaways
- connect the findings to implications
- preserve technical precision

## 3. Briefing-deck workflow

For stakeholder or strategy decks:

- foreground decisions and implications
- reduce methodological depth
- focus on action, risk, and next steps

## 4. Meeting-to-deck workflow

When a meeting or discussion needs a deck:

1. use Meeting Memory outputs
2. identify decisions and unresolved issues
3. convert them into a structured briefing sequence

## 5. PhD-to-deck workflow

When the presentation is PhD-related:

1. align with the thesis spine
2. use the article map or central note
3. make clear where the presented material fits in the broader program

## 6. Revision workflow

Presentation Maker should review decks for:

- narrative gaps
- repetition
- too much detail
- unclear takeaway slides
- weak conclusions
