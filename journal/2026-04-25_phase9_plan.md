- `.kicker--accent` — slow 8s left→right shimmer on key sections (Today brief, Knowledge slipcase header).
- `.feed-row` — already animates; add subtle accent left-bar slide-in on hover.
- `.htmx-indicator` — global skeleton fade-out (180ms) when partials swap in.
- Sidebar nav-item — accent-bar slide-in (already supported, refine timing curve).
