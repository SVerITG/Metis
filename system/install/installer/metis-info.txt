  WHAT IS METIS?
  ──────────────
  Metis is an AI research companion that runs alongside Claude. It
  gives every AI conversation a persistent memory of your domain,
  your papers, your projects, and your working history.

  The longer you use it, the better every response gets — not because
  the AI changes, but because Metis knows you better.

  You don't need to follow developments in AI. Metis does that for
  you. Every week it reviews its own performance, identifies where it
  could do better, and proposes improvements. You approve them. Your
  research stays your focus.


  WHAT THIS INSTALLER DOES
  ─────────────────────────
  Step 1  Sets up Python (required to run Metis)
  Step 2  Installs Metis packages — may take 5-15 minutes
  Step 3  Loads demo content (if you chose the demo option)
  Step 4  Builds your knowledge index from included documents
  Step 5  Personalises Metis to your research profile
  Step 6  Registers Metis with Claude Desktop and Claude Code


  WHAT IS AN MCP SERVER?
  ────────────────────────
  MCP (Model Context Protocol) is an open standard from Anthropic.
  Metis uses it to give Claude a persistent connection to your
  research world — your papers, your projects, your notes, your
  meeting history. The MCP server is a small background process.
  You will never interact with it directly — it just works.


  HOW YOU WILL KNOW METIS IS WORKING
  ────────────────────────────────────
  Once installed, open Claude Desktop or Claude Code. Metis will:

    • Remember what you were working on in your last session
    • Know your research domain and your terminology
    • Route your questions to the right specialist automatically
      (Epidemiologist, Writing Partner, Librarian, Methods Coach,
       and 30+ more)
    • Cross-reference your ideas with your literature automatically
    • Build a morning brief of new papers overnight


  YOUR DATA IS PROTECTED
  ────────────────────────
  Everything stays on your machine:
    ✓  Your PDF library and indexed documents
    ✓  Your meeting notes and transcripts
    ✓  Your ideas, journal, and task lists
    ✓  Your project files and folder contents
    ✓  Your API key (stored in a local .env file only)
    ✓  All voice recordings

  What leaves your machine (and when):
    →  Text you send to Claude for analysis
       (same as typing in Claude Desktop directly)
    →  Your configured research search terms, sent to PubMed/OpenAlex
       once per day for the morning brief (optional, can be disabled)
    →  Zotero library metadata if you enable sync (optional)

  When project folders are scanned, you choose exactly what Metis
  reads: file names only, README/notes, or nothing (you describe it).
  Metis never reads patient data — it is detected and blocked.


  TOKEN USAGE AND COSTS
  ──────────────────────
  Metis uses the Anthropic API (your API key). Each agent run shows
  exactly what it cost — which specialist was used, how many tokens,
  at what rate. The dashboard has a live token pulse showing your
  daily usage. Most daily research tasks cost a few cents.
  You stay in full control of what runs and when.
