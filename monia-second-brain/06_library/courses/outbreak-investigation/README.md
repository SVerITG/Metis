# Outbreak Investigation

This course introduces the practical workflow of outbreak investigation for epidemiologists, field teams, and public health decision-makers. It combines classic field epidemiology logic with operational realities such as verification, line listing, analytic studies, laboratory coordination, control measures, and after-action review.

## Audience
- Early-career field epidemiologists
- Public health surveillance officers
- Clinicians and programme staff supporting outbreak response

## Structure
- 10 lessons
- Starts with confirmation and preparation
- Moves through descriptive and analytic investigation
- Ends with control, communication, and post-outbreak learning

## Core resources
- CDC Principles of Epidemiology and Field Epidemiology Manual
- WHO outbreak investigation and rapid risk assessment guidance
- ECDC operational public health resources
- Related Metis cards in `06_library/methods/` and `06_library/concepts/`
