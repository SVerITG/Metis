# PhD Domain

This folder is the long-term home for your PhD knowledge and planning material.

## Recommended usage

- `00_center`: the central unifying document and adjacent framing notes
- `01_current-articles`: the three current papers and their support material
- `02_future-articles`: candidate papers, ideas, and exploratory directions
- `03_methods`: methods to learn, methodological notes, and analytical gaps
- `04_notes`: general PhD notes and conceptual writing
- `05_meetings`: supervisory and research meetings related to the PhD
- `06_sources`: source material specific to the PhD domain

## Central file

Put the document that brings the current papers together in:

- `00_center/`

That central document should become the anchor for:

- the unifying topic
- the narrative connecting the current articles
- candidate future papers
- methodological needs
- open conceptual questions
