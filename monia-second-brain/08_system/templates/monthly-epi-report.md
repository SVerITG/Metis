# Monthly Epidemiology Report

> Template for strategic monthly reflection: context, progress toward goals, deeper analysis, and medium-term planning.

## Reporting month

- **Month:** `YYYY-MM`
- **Compiled by:** `[name/agent]`
- **Distribution list:** (Control Room, PH leadership, Agents)

## Snapshot highlights

- **Top achievement/insight:** (brief narrative)  
- **Key metric(s):** (cases, elimination milestones, surveillance coverage, news volume)  
- **Emerging challenge:** (risk area needing planning)

## Deep dives

### 1. Systemic trends
- Narrative summary of major trends (epidemics, policy shifts, global health signals)  
- Charts or tables (link to data stored in Metis)  
- Link to Metis cards or projects impacted

### 2. Competency & learning linkage
- Which Metis courses/cards were surfaced, updated, or prompted action this month  
- Training opportunities or knowledge gaps  
- Suggested new cards/resources

### 3. Research & evidence pipeline
- Noteworthy studies released this month (with citations)  
- Status of ongoing projects (analysis, submissions)  
- Data/products needing review (surveys, dashboards)

## Operations

- **Events attended/planned:** (conferences, town halls)  
- **Agent assignments:** (who’s covering which knowledge nodes)  
- **Infrastructure notes:** (database refreshes, template changes)

## Next 30 days

- **Focus areas:** (surveillance, elimination, writing, ethics, digital)  
- **Milestones to deliver:** (reports, courses, briefings)  
- **Risks to mitigate:** (funding, geopolitics, data gaps)

## Appendices

- **Data sources referenced:** list  
- **Linked README/templates:** reference  
- **Glossary updates:** mention new terms added  

---

*Use this to align monthly reflection with Metis’s education, surveillance, and research priorities; keep total length <1,200 words.*
