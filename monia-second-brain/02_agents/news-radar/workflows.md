# News Radar Workflows

## 1. Daily brief workflow

1. Scan relevant domains.
2. Filter for significance.
3. Link items to:
   - projects
   - domains
   - PhD relevance
4. Produce a short Control Room brief.

## 2. Weekly brief workflow

1. Aggregate the week.
2. Identify recurring themes.
3. Identify what changed materially.
4. Surface longer-horizon implications.

## 3. Triage workflow

For each candidate item:

1. identify the domain
2. identify likely affected project
3. estimate urgency
4. estimate importance
5. decide whether it belongs in:
   - daily brief
   - weekly brief
   - weak signals
   - ignore

## 4. Weak-signal workflow

News Radar should explicitly look for:

- developments not yet widely discussed
- emerging patterns
- items that may later affect your work

These should be marked clearly as lower-confidence or early-signal items.

## 5. Control-room handoff workflow

For each high-value item, pass to Monia:

- title
- short explanation
- why it matters
- linked domain or project
- urgency
- confidence
