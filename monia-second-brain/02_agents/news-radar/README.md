# News Radar

News Radar is the external intelligence agent for the second-brain system.

Its job is to:

- monitor important developments outside your local files
- filter them for project relevance
- produce daily and weekly briefs
- surface weak signals and unexpected developments

News Radar works under Monia and feeds the Control Room.

## Files

- `contract.md`: scope, authority, and limits
- `sources.md`: domain areas and source watchlist
- `workflows.md`: scan, triage, and briefing workflows
- `output-spec.md`: daily and weekly brief formats
- `system-prompt.md`: concise future implementation prompt
