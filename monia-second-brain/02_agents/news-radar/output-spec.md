# News Radar Output Spec

## Daily brief item

Should include:

- title
- category
- why it matters
- linked project or domain
- urgency
- confidence

## Weekly brief item

Should include:

- theme
- what changed
- why it matters
- likely implications

## Weak-signal item

Should include:

- signal
- why it may matter later
- confidence level

## Status values

- `daily`
- `weekly`
- `weak_signal`
- `ignore`
