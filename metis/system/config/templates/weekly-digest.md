# Weekly News Digest

> Template for summarizing trends, emerging risks, and operational notes across the week. Use to update the Control Room newsletter and align teams.

## Week ending

- **Period:** `YYYY-MM-DD` to `YYYY-MM-DD`
- **Compiled by:** `[name/agent]`

## Executive summary

- **Headline trend:** (1-2 sentences capturing dominant story)
- **Risk level:** (Low / Moderate / High) + rationale
- **Strategic implication:** (e.g., accelerate surveillance, revisit protocols)

## Thematic sections

### 1. Surveillance & epidemiology
- Key statistics (cases, outbreaks, surveillance coverage)  
- Notable geographic or population shifts  
- Source links

### 2. Policy & guidance
- New or updated WHO/CDC/ECDC guidance  
- Regulatory/news policy from ministries  
- Implications for Metis stakeholders

### 3. Research & method updates
- New papers, tools, or training releases  
- Relevance to Metis courses or cards  
- Suggested follow-up

### 4. Signals to monitor
- `Signal` | `Why it matters` | `Responsible lead` | `Next check-in`
- Use table format for clarity.

## Actions & requests

- **What we committed to:** List any tasks assigned or decisions pending.  
- **Evidence needed:** Specify data, papers, or briefing material required.  
- **Next stakeholder touchpoints:** (meetings, briefings)

## Sources & feeds

- Provide a bulleted list of news feeds, journals, policy sites consulted (with URLs where possible).

---

*Keep weekly digests under 750 words; send to Control Room leads every Friday or agreed cadence.*
