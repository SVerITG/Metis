# NTD Elimination Strategies

This course introduces how neglected tropical disease elimination programmes move from control to interruption, verification, and post-elimination stewardship. It emphasizes strategy design, surveillance, integration, equity, operational feasibility, and the reality that elimination is a systems challenge as much as a technical one.

## Audience
- Epidemiologists working on NTD control or elimination
- Programme staff involved in surveillance and strategy design
- Researchers studying endgame and post-elimination policy

## Structure
- 8 lessons
- Starts with elimination concepts and targets
- Moves through intervention strategy, surveillance, and verification
- Ends with sustainability, integration, and post-elimination risk

## Core resources
- WHO NTD road map and disease-specific guidance
- Elimination and post-elimination surveillance resources
- Related Metis cards in `knowledge/library/methods/` and `knowledge/library/concepts/`
