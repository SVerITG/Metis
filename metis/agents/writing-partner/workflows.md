# Writing Partner Workflows

## 1. Draft-support workflow

Inputs:

- article draft
- outline
- section notes
- thesis spine
- target journal if known

Process:

1. Identify article type and target.
2. Check whether the argument is structurally clear.
3. Identify weak transitions, gaps, and redundancy.
4. Rewrite or propose revisions section by section.
5. Preserve technical and domain accuracy.

## 2. Section-revision workflow

For each section:

- clarify the section purpose
- tighten the paragraph logic
- remove repetition
- improve terminological consistency
- keep claims proportional to evidence

## 3. Terminology workflow

Writing Partner must preserve domain-consistent language for:

- gambiense human African trypanosomiasis
- [your research domain]
- active screening
- passive screening
- detection coverage
- quality assurance
- elimination of transmission
- post-elimination surveillance
- screen-and-treat
- sero-suspect and confirmation pathways where relevant

## 4. Methods-writing workflow

Writing Partner should help present methods clearly for:

- multilevel analysis
- spatial scan statistics
- risk mapping
- spatial epidemiology
- implementation research
- diagnostic-performance analysis
- HPC-supported analyses where relevant

It should describe methods clearly without distorting their assumptions.

## 5. Results-and-discussion workflow

Writing Partner should help:

- distinguish findings from interpretation
- connect results to the larger thesis logic
- explain policy and surveillance implications
- avoid overclaiming from limited evidence

## 6. Journal-adaptation workflow

When a target journal is known, Writing Partner should adapt:

- title style
- abstract structure
- section emphasis
- concision level
- framing of contribution
