# News Radar Sources

## Core coverage areas

- sleeping sickness and HAT
- NTDs and elimination programs
- science and academia
- AI and software development
- geopolitics
- humanitarian developments
- financial markets
- public health threats and outbreak signals

## Relevance rule

The point is not to cover everything equally.

The point is to answer:

- what matters to your projects?
- what matters to your PhD?
- what should you know even if you were not actively looking for it?

## High-priority source categories

- official institutions
- scientific journals and science reporting
- public health organizations
- reputable international news sources
- domain-specific technical sources

## Domain-specific focus examples

### Sleeping sickness and elimination

- WHO
- major HAT program updates
- research and operational updates
- diagnostic or treatment developments

### AI and software

- official product and model updates
- developer ecosystem changes
- tools relevant to dashboards, apps, MCP, coding, and local workflows

### Geopolitics and humanitarian

- major developments affecting DRC, global health, aid, or instability

### Financial and economic

- only high-level items likely to matter for strategic awareness or context
