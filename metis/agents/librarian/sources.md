- PubMed
- Europe PMC

## Preprint and repository sources

- medRxiv

## Programmatic and institutional sources

- WHO HAT pages

- NGO, donor, and operational program reports

## Preferred source behavior

- Prefer authoritative and primary sources first.
- Use reviews to orient, not replace primary evidence.
- Track both journal papers and operational reports.
